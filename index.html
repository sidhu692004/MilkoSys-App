MilkoSys Dairy

MilkoSys Dairy is designed for dairy businesses to simplify daily operations. It helps in managing customer orders, billing, payments, and stock in one place. With this app, dairy owners can save time, avoid errors, and grow their business digitally.

Key Features:

  • Manage customers & orders
  • Automated billing & receipts
  • Track inventory & stock
  • Reports & insights for growth

MilkoSys (User)

MilkoSys (User) app connects customers directly with local dairies. Users can order fresh milk, ghee, curd, and other dairy products from the comfort of their home. The app ensures fast delivery, transparent pricing, and secure payments.

Key Features:

  • Easy dairy product ordering
  • Track order & delivery
  • Multiple payment options
  • Discounts & offers

MilkoSys Hero

MilkoSys Hero is built for delivery boys (heroes) who make sure dairy products reach customers on time. It provides an easy way to view assigned orders, update delivery status, and collect payments. With this app, delivery becomes smooth and well-organized.

Key Features:

  • View daily delivery tasks
  • Navigation & route optimization
  • Update order status (Delivered/Pending)
  • Cash & online payment support
